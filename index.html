Frequently Asked Questions

The cost of a dental implant varies depending on several factors including the type of implant, any additional procedures needed (like bone grafting), and the type of crown used. On average, a single dental implant in our clinic ranges from ₹25,000 to ₹40,000. We offer free consultations where we can provide you with an exact estimate based on your specific needs.

Modern root canal treatments are performed with advanced anesthesia techniques, making the procedure virtually painless. You might experience some mild discomfort after the procedure as the anesthesia wears off, but this can be managed with over-the-counter pain medication. Our clinic uses dental microscopes for precision and comfort during the procedure.

Professional teeth whitening results typically last between 6 months to 2 years, depending on your eating, drinking, and oral hygiene habits. Avoiding staining substances like coffee, tea, red wine, and tobacco can help maintain your results longer. We also offer take-home whitening kits for touch-ups.

Veneers are thin shells that cover only the front surface of teeth to improve appearance, while crowns encase the entire tooth to restore both function and appearance. Veneers are typically used for cosmetic purposes on healthy teeth, while crowns are used to protect and strengthen damaged or weakened teeth. During your consultation, we can recommend which option is best for your specific situation.

Yes, we accept most major dental insurance plans. We'll be happy to check your coverage and help you understand your benefits. Please bring your insurance card when you visit, and we'll handle the paperwork for you. For procedures not covered by insurance, we offer flexible payment plans to make treatment affordable.
